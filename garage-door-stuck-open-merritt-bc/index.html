Why a garage door may stay open

A garage door stuck open may involve opener or sensor issues, a track obstruction, damaged rollers, broken springs, snapped cables, or weather-related movement. These symptoms should be described clearly during intake without guessing at the final cause.

If the door is crooked, noisy, or the opener strains, stop repeated attempts. For broader urgent access or safety concerns, use the emergency garage door repair request page.

Tell the intake line whether the door is fully open or partly open, whether the opener runs, whether the remote or wall button works, and whether anything is visible in the tracks.

Garage door stuck open FAQ

What should I do if my garage door is stuck open?

Avoid forcing it. Call to submit a request and describe the opener, track, sensor, cable, spring, and security concerns you can see safely.

Is service guaranteed?

No. Provider availability, emergency availability, accepted jobs, response times, pricing, and scheduling vary.

Should I disconnect the opener?

Do not disconnect or pull on the door if it may be damaged, heavy, crooked, or under uneven tension. Wait for provider guidance when possible.

Who performs the work?

An independent provider performs any accepted work, not Garage Door Request Help.
