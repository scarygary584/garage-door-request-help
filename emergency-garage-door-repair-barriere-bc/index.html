Safety note: Stay clear of springs, cables, tracks, rollers, and a door that is crooked or unsupported. If there is immediate danger, injury risk, fire, vehicle impact damage, or a security emergency, contact emergency services or the appropriate professional first.

When a garage door problem becomes urgent

A garage door stuck open can leave a home exposed to weather, animals, theft risk, or overnight security concerns. A door stuck closed can trap a vehicle or block access. If the door looks crooked, has a loose cable, or will not move evenly, avoid forcing it.

During intake, describe the Barriere-area location, whether the door is open or closed, whether a vehicle is trapped, whether the opener still runs, and whether springs, cables, tracks, or panels look damaged.
