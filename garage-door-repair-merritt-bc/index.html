Common Merritt garage door repair requests

Residential overhead doors can stop working for many reasons. A stuck garage door may involve tracks, rollers, opener settings, springs, cables, hinges, weather, or obstruction. A provider determines diagnosis and repair options after review.

Broken springs and snapped cables are especially important to describe because they can leave the door too heavy to lift safely. If the opener strains but the door does not move, stop repeated attempts and mention that symptom during intake.

Other garage door repair Merritt BC requests include doors that will not close, doors that reverse unexpectedly, noisy or shaking movement, bent track concerns, weatherstripping problems, damaged panels, and basic residential overhead door issues.
