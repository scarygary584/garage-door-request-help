Safety note: Do not force a stuck-open garage door if it is crooked, heavy, jammed, or has loose cables. Avoid standing underneath a damaged or unsupported door.

What can cause a door to stay open?

A garage door may stay open because of blocked sensors, opener settings, track obstruction, broken rollers, snapped cable, broken spring, damaged panels, weather exposure, or a door that is binding in the tracks.

During intake, mention whether the opener runs, whether the door reverses, whether the sensors blink, whether the door is crooked, and whether the garage is exposed or unsecured.

Garage door stuck open FAQ

Is a garage door stuck open an emergency?

It can be urgent if the home is unsecured, exposed to weather, or access is blocked. Emergency availability and response times are not guaranteed.

Should I force the door closed?

No. Avoid forcing a damaged, crooked, heavy, or jammed door because springs, cables, and tracks can create injury risk.
