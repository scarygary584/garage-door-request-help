Spring warning signs

  • ✓Visible gap in the spring or a loud bang from the garage
  • ✓Door feels extremely heavy or only opens a few inches
  • ✓Opener strains, hums, or jerks while the door stays down
Safety note: Garage door springs and cables are under tension. Do not loosen, remove, cut, or adjust spring parts. Avoid standing under or forcing a heavy or crooked door.

What to mention during intake

Tell the intake line whether the door is stuck open or closed, whether a vehicle is trapped, whether the opener still runs, and whether you see a spring gap, loose cable, crooked door, or damaged track.

Spring systems can make the door too heavy to lift safely. A provider determines the diagnosis, pricing, scheduling, repair work, warranties, and service terms for any accepted job.
