Common garage door problems in Merritt homes

Broken springs or snapped cables

A loud bang, heavy door, visible spring gap, or loose cable can signal a serious tension-system concern. Do not attempt DIY spring repair.

Garage door opener issues

Openers may hum, click, strain, reverse unexpectedly, or fail to respond to remotes, keypads, or wall buttons.

Stuck or crooked overhead doors

Doors can stick from track concerns, broken rollers, cable issues, obstruction, weather exposure, or impact damage.

Noisy or shaking doors

Grinding, scraping, shaking, or uneven movement can be worth reporting before forcing the door or repeatedly using the opener.
